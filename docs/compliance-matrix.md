# Multilingual obligations review

This open-source local reporting tool is not itself a government service and has
no named public-entity deployment. No universal law requires this repository to
offer Spanish output. EN/ES support is a product and equity decision for human-
services organizations, not a legal-compliance claim.

An adopting organization must determine its own funder contract, Title VI/LEP,
state/local language-access, Section 508, ADA Title II, and procurement duties.
The generated artifact carries a valid language tag and uses the same grounding
and suppression enforcement in both shipping languages. That technical parity
does not replace professional translation or legal review.

*Last verified: 2026-07-12 · Recheck: annually and on a named public deployment.*
